More Ways to Get Help 🌟

👨‍⚕️
Talk to a School Counselor

Your school counselor is trained to help students with stress, anxiety, and personal problems. Their job is to help you — and it's confidential.

💙 Tip: You can write a note to your counselor if talking feels hard.
👪
Talk to a Trusted Adult

It could be a parent, relative, teacher, or neighbour. Sometimes just saying your problem out loud to someone you trust makes it feel 50% smaller.

💚 You don't have to have all the answers. Just say "I'm struggling and I need support."
🧘
Try These Free Apps

Free mental health apps can help you every day:

📱 Headspace — Meditation & mindfulness
📱 Calm — Sleep & relaxation
📱 Wysa — AI emotional support
📱 Youper — Mood tracker & CBT
